# Notes
### M.2 Drives
* Mostly a form factor.
* Drives are 22mm wide. Can have different lengths like 42, 60, or 80 mm, leading to sizes like 2280.
* Different keying patterns for the pins: B, M, B+M.
* Older drives SATA. Newer drives NVMe.
